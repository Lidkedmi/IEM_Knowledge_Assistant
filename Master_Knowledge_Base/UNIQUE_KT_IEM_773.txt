KNOWLEDGE TRANSFER DOCUMENT: IEM_773
====================================
1. תמצית: System failure investigation concludes that a central conveyor belt collapsed due to material fatigue in a bearing.
2. רקע: The root cause analysis of the central conveyor belt collapse on 12.05 identified material fatigue in a bearing that was not replaced during the last preventive maintenance.
3. תובנה מרכזית: Preventive maintenance schedules must be updated to include critical component replacements based on material fatigue analysis, and advanced monitoring systems like vibration sensors can detect early signs of failure.
4. צעדים:
   * Update the preventive maintenance checklist to include bearing replacement.
   * Install vibration sensors on critical motors.
   * Refresh and reinforce weekly lubrication procedures.
   * Review maintenance logs for other critical components.
5. אזהרות:
   * Neglecting scheduled replacement of critical components can lead to catastrophic failures.
   * Regularly monitor vibration data for early detection of bearing wear.
   * Ensure all maintenance personnel are aware of updated procedures.
