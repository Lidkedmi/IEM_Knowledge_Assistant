====================================

3. תובנה מרכזית: Prompt detection and immediate response to critical equipment failures, especially hydraulic leaks, are essential to minimize product damage, prevent further machine breakdown, and ensure operational safety.
4. צעדים:
   * Stop production on line 4 immediately.
   * Maintenance team to arrive with a replacement seal kit within 15 minutes.
   * Repair the hydraulic leak in the main cylinder.
5. אזהרות:
   * Do not resume work until explicit approval from the shift manager is received.
