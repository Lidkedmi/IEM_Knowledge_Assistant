KNOWLEDGE TRANSFER DOCUMENT: IEM_601
====================================
1. תמצית: Production line 4 experienced a 6-hour shutdown due to a main engine failure caused by insufficient lubrication.
2. רקע: A critical production line suffered a significant downtime event due to a preventable mechanical failure, highlighting a gap in preventive maintenance.
3. תובנה מרכזית: Inadequate preventive maintenance frequency, specifically lubrication, directly leads to critical equipment failure and substantial production downtime; continuous monitoring and adjusted maintenance schedules are crucial.
4. צעדים:
   * Replaced the main engine.
   * Updated the preventive maintenance schedule to weekly lubrication instead of monthly.
   * Installed a temperature sensor for continuous monitoring of the engine.
5. אזהרות:
   * Insufficient lubrication can cause catastrophic equipment failure and prolonged production stoppages.
   * Unmonitored critical components pose a high risk of unexpected and costly downtime.
   * Failure to update maintenance plans based on root cause analysis will lead to recurrence of similar failures.
