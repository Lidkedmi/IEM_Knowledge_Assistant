KNOWLEDGE TRANSFER DOCUMENT: IEM_499
====================================
1. תמצית: An attempted external cyber attack on the production network (OT systems) was detected, prompting an immediate security response.
2. רקע: Unusual activity, identified as an attempted external cyber attack, was detected on the operational technology (OT) network, posing a significant threat to production systems.
3. תובנה מרכזית: OT systems are vulnerable to external cyber threats, requiring immediate and decisive action to isolate systems and prevent potential damage or disruption to production.
4. צעדים:
   * Immediately disconnect all workstations from the internet.
   * Switch to offline mode for operations until further notice.
   * Notify the cyber security team for a full system scan.
   * Shift managers must ensure compliance with these directives.
5. אזהרות:
   * Failure to promptly disconnect from the internet during a cyber attack can lead to system compromise and severe operational disruption.
   * Operating online during an active attack can expose critical OT systems to further damage.
   * Treat all unusual network activity in OT environments as a potential security breach.
