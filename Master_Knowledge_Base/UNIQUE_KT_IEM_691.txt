KNOWLEDGE TRANSFER DOCUMENT: IEM_691
====================================
1. תמצית: Production line 4 was emergency stopped due to a main engine overheating and shaft fracture, requiring a 24-hour shutdown.

3. תובנה מרכזית: Sudden critical equipment failure (like a main engine shaft fracture) necessitates immediate line shutdown and rapid reallocation of urgent orders to maintain production continuity.
4. צעדים:
   * Production line 4 stopped at 08:15 due to main engine (M-202) overheating.
   * Maintenance technicians identified a fractured shaft.
   * The line will be down for the next 24 hours.
   * Immediately transfer all urgent orders to line 3.
   * Refer to the attached initial fault report.
5. אזהרות:
   * Risk of further damage if the engine is operated before repair.
   * Potential delays in fulfilling urgent orders if line 3 capacity is insufficient.
