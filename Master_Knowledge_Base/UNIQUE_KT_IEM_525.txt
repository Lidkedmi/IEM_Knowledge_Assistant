KNOWLEDGE TRANSFER DOCUMENT: IEM_525
====================================
1. תמצית: A new preventive maintenance procedure for hydraulic presses has been implemented following recent breakdowns.
2. רקע: A series of hydraulic press breakdowns occurred in the past month, indicating a need for improved and more frequent maintenance practices.
3. תובנה מרכזית: Proactive and more frequent preventive maintenance, including stricter component replacement schedules and diligent logging, is crucial to prevent costly machine breakdowns and ensure operational continuity.
4. צעדים:
   * Perform daily morning oil pressure checks on all hydraulic presses.
   * Replace seals every 2,000 cycles (reduced from 5,000 cycles).
   * Record all maintenance activities and checks in the digital machine log.
5. אזהרות:
   * Non-compliance with the new procedure will result in immediate machine shutdown.
   * Ensure all operators and maintenance staff are thoroughly trained on the new procedure and its importance.
   * Monitor breakdown frequency to assess the effectiveness of the new procedure and make further adjustments if needed.
