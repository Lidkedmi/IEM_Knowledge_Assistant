KNOWLEDGE TRANSFER DOCUMENT: IEM_440
====================================
1. תמצית: An annual fire safety audit revealed three emergency exits in the hazardous materials warehouse were blocked by pallets.
2. רקע: A critical annual safety audit identified severe fire safety violations, posing immediate danger in a hazardous materials storage area.
3. תובנה מרכזית: Maintaining clear emergency exits is a non-negotiable safety requirement, especially in hazardous material zones, and demands continuous, proactive monitoring to prevent catastrophic incidents.
4. צעדים:
   * Immediately clear all blocked emergency exits.
   * Appoint a 'Safety Trustee' responsible for daily safety checks.
   * Implement daily morning safety scans by the Safety Trustee across all critical areas.
   * Ensure adherence to the attached safety scanning protocol.
5. אזהרות:
   * Blocked emergency exits are a first-degree safety failure and can lead to severe injuries, fatalities, or operational shutdowns during an emergency.
   * Failure to implement daily checks can result in recurring safety violations and a false sense of security.
   * Non-compliance with safety protocols can lead to significant legal liabilities and regulatory penalties.
