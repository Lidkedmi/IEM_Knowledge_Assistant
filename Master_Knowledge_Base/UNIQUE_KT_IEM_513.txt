KNOWLEDGE TRANSFER DOCUMENT: IEM_513
====================================
1. תמצית: A serious safety incident occurred where a pallet fell from 6 meters due to a faulty forklift, leading to new safety protocols.
2. רקע: This information is documented following a severe safety incident to formalize the investigation's findings and implement corrective actions to prevent recurrence and enhance workplace safety.
3. תובנה מרכזית: Thorough investigation of safety incidents reveals critical operational flaws, emphasizing the need for strict equipment maintenance, adherence to load limits, and technological safeguards to prevent serious accidents.
4. צעדים:
   * Mandatory daily operational checks for all forklifts.
   * Enforce a maximum load limit of 800 kg on standard wooden pallets.
   * Utilize new weight sensors installed in the forklift fleet to prevent overloads.
   * Train forklift operators on new procedures and sensor usage.
   * Review and update warehouse safety protocols.
5. אזהרות:
   * Using faulty equipment poses severe safety risks and can lead to serious accidents.
   * Exceeding load limits on pallets or equipment can cause structural failure and falls.
   * Lack of daily equipment checks can allow critical malfunctions to go unnoticed until an incident occurs.
