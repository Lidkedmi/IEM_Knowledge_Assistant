KNOWLEDGE TRANSFER DOCUMENT: IEM_775
====================================
1. תמצית: Severe quality deviation in electronics components shipment #992, with 12% failing voltage specifications.
2. רקע: Incoming Quality Control (IQC) inspection of a shipment from 'Techno-Component' revealed that 12% of the components do not meet the required voltage specifications.
3. תובנה מרכזית: Thorough incoming quality control is vital to prevent the use of non-conforming components, which can lead to production line stoppages and product failures. Supplier performance must be actively managed.
4. צעדים:
   * Immediately halt the use of this component batch in all production lines.
   * Coordinate with the purchasing department to return the defective goods.
   * Request an urgent replacement shipment from the supplier.
   * Review supplier quality performance and consider corrective actions.
5. אזהרות:
   * Using non-conforming components can lead to product defects and customer dissatisfaction.
   * Ensure clear communication with suppliers regarding quality requirements.
   * Implement robust IQC procedures for all critical components.
