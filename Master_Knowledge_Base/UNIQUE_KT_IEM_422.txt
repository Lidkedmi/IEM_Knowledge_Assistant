KNOWLEDGE TRANSFER DOCUMENT: IEM_422
====================================
1. תמצית: An investigation into a raw material supply delay revealed an EDI interface failure with a German supplier.
2. רקע: Raw material supply was delayed last week due to an identified failure in the Electronic Data Interchange (EDI) interface with a key supplier in Germany.
3. תובנה מרכזית: Over-reliance on automated systems like EDI without manual oversight can lead to critical supply chain disruptions; regular manual checks are essential for critical interfaces.
4. צעדים:
   * Conduct a manual control check of the EDI interface with the German supplier every Thursday morning.
5. אזהרות:
   * EDI interface failures can cause significant raw material supply delays.
   * Do not assume automated systems are infallible; implement regular manual verification for critical processes.
