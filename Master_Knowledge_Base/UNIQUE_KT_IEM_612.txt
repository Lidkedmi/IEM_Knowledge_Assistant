KNOWLEDGE TRANSFER DOCUMENT: IEM_612
====================================
1. תמצית: A new procedure for counting Work-In-Process (WIP) inventory at the end of each shift will be implemented to reduce discrepancies between ERP and physical stock.
2. רקע: This document introduces a new operational procedure aimed at improving inventory accuracy and data integrity between physical stock and the ERP system.
3. תובנה מרכזית: Regular and accurate physical inventory counts, integrated with digital systems, are essential for maintaining data integrity, reducing discrepancies, and improving overall inventory management efficiency.
4. צעדים:
   * Implement the new procedure for Work-In-Process (WIP) inventory counting at the end of every shift.
   * Each team leader must accurately enter the WIP data into the shared SharePoint table.
   * Ensure data entry into the SharePoint table is completed by 07:00 AM daily.
   * Refer to the attached step-by-step guide for proper completion of the table.
5. אזהרות:
   * Ensure all team leaders and relevant personnel are adequately trained on the new procedure and the SharePoint data entry process.
   * Monitor data accuracy and timeliness rigorously to prevent the emergence of new discrepancies between physical and system inventory.
   * Verify that the attached guide is clear, comprehensive, and addresses all potential questions regarding the new process.
