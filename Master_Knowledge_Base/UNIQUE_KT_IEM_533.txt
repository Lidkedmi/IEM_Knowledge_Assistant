KNOWLEDGE TRANSFER DOCUMENT: IEM_533
====================================
1. תמצית: A delay in component supply for Project Alpha was caused by a lack of coordination between purchasing and the shipping agent.
2. רקע: This information is being documented to prevent future supply chain delays and improve coordination with external logistics partners, following a root cause analysis.
3. תובנה מרכזית: Effective supply chain management requires robust coordination mechanisms and proactive monitoring of critical suppliers and logistics partners to prevent costly project delays.
4. צעדים:
   * Implement weekly tracking with critical suppliers.
   * Utilize an early warning system for supply chain disruptions.
   * Improve coordination protocols between the purchasing department and shipping agents.
5. אזהרות:
   * Failure to maintain regular communication with critical suppliers can lead to unforeseen delays.
   * Lack of a proactive alert system can result in late detection of supply chain issues, impacting project timelines and costs.
