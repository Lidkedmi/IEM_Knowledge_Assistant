KNOWLEDGE TRANSFER DOCUMENT: IEM_563
====================================
1. תמצית: A systemic supply chain failure due to lack of strategic supplier redundancy caused raw material delays.
2. רקע: An investigation was conducted following a delay in raw material supply from China last month.
3. תובנה מרכזית: Relying on a single strategic supplier without redundancy poses a significant risk to supply chain stability and operational continuity, necessitating diversification.
4. צעדים:
   * Establish a database of alternative suppliers in Europe.
   * Procurement manager to present a list of alternative suppliers by the 30th of the month.
5. אזהרות:
   * Failure to diversify strategic suppliers can lead to severe production delays and financial losses.
   * Ensure alternative suppliers meet quality, cost, and reliability standards before integration.
