KNOWLEDGE TRANSFER DOCUMENT: IEM_772
====================================
1. תמצית: New procedure for Work-In-Progress (WIP) inventory reporting in the ERP system.
2. רקע: Starting next Sunday, a new procedure requires barcode scanning at each station transition on the assembly line to improve the accuracy of production cost calculations.
3. תובנה מרכזית: Accurate and timely WIP reporting via barcode scanning is essential for precise production cost accounting and efficient production order closure.
4. צעדים:
   * Scan the work order at each station.
   * Confirm the quantity of good parts.
   * Report any scrap or defects.
   * Ensure all scans are completed before the end of the shift to allow for work order closure.
5. אזהרות:
   * Failure to report WIP accurately will prevent work order closure at the end of the shift.
   * Ensure all assembly line personnel are trained on the new scanning procedure.
   * Verify that barcode scanners are functioning correctly.
