KNOWLEDGE TRANSFER DOCUMENT: IEM_502
====================================
1. תמצית: A new Kanban inventory management procedure will be implemented in the spare parts warehouse starting this Sunday.
2. רקע: To optimize inventory management in the spare parts warehouse, a new Kanban system is being introduced to streamline reordering and reduce stockouts.
3. תובנה מרכזית: Implementing a pull-based inventory system like Kanban can significantly improve efficiency, reduce waste, and ensure timely replenishment of critical spare parts.
4. צעדים:
   * Mark reorder points clearly on shelves for each item.
   * When an item reaches its reorder point, fill out a demand card in the ERP system.
   * Ensure the purchasing department is updated promptly based on ERP demand cards.
   * Familiarize with the detailed steps and use of Kanban cards (e.g., yellow cards) as per the attached PDF.
5. אזהרות:
   * Incorrectly marking reorder points or failing to process demand cards promptly can lead to stockouts or overstocking.
   * Lack of proper training or adherence to the new Kanban procedure can undermine its effectiveness.
   * Initial implementation may require close monitoring to fine-tune reorder points and card flow.
