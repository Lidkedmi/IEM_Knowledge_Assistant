KNOWLEDGE TRANSFER DOCUMENT: IEM_918
====================================
1. תמצית: A cross-functional project successfully reduced production costs on Line A by 18%, resulting in an annual saving of 1.2 million ILS.
2. רקע: The company initiated a project to reduce production costs on Line A, aiming for improved efficiency and profitability.
3. תובנה מרכזית: Holistic cost reduction projects involving process optimization, supply chain adjustments, and waste reduction, supported by inter-departmental collaboration, can yield significant financial savings and operational efficiencies.
4. צעדים:
   * Optimize assembly processes on production line A.
   * Evaluate and switch to alternative raw material suppliers (e.g., B-Tech).
   * Implement measures to reduce production waste (e.g., by 7%).
   * Foster strong collaboration between engineering, procurement, and production departments.
5. אזהרות:
   * Ensure new suppliers meet all quality standards and specifications before full transition.
   * Monitor the long-term impact of process changes on product quality and employee morale.
   * Verify that cost reductions do not compromise safety or product performance.
